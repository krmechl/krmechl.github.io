About Dr. Janet Stout:

THE LEGIONELLA EXPERTS®

The Legionella Experts® are led by Dr. Janet Stout, an infectious disease microbiologist who is internationally renowned for more than 30 years of pioneering research and expertise in Legionella detection, prevention, and control. A self-described “Legionellologist,” she is founder of Special Pathogens Technology, executive vice president and founder of Special Pathogens Laboratory, and research associate professor in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh Swanson School of Engineering.
